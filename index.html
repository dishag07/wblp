Meet the Owner

Hi, I'm Neha Gupta, the proud owner of Accounting Tax USA based in Marlboro, New Jersey. I'm a licensed CPA with a Bachelor's in Finance. I built this firm from the ground up through word-of-mouth referrals and the support of my family and team. Over the past four years, our focus on reliable, client-first service has helped us grow and thrive.

My goal was to create a transparent, flat-fee, full-service firm with no hidden costs. At Accounting Tax USA, we offer responsive planning, fast turnarounds, and clear answers—whether you're filing personal, business, or cannabis-related returns.
