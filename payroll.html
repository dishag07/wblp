What is a Payroll Service?

A payroll service manages employee payments and employment tax filings on behalf of a business. This includes:

  • Gathering employee wage and hour data
  • Calculating gross wages and tax deductions
  • Issuing paychecks or direct deposits
  • Filing required tax documents

Each client gets a custom account to track employee hours, benefits, and compliance. On payday, employees receive their pay, and the business receives a detailed payroll and tax report.
