Estate and Trust

Managing estate and trust finances can be time-consuming and complex, but the experienced team at Accounting Tax USA is here to help. We handle all general accounting duties, ensuring accurate records, proper tax filings, and correct distributions to beneficiaries. Working closely with your attorney, insurance agent, and investment advisor, we analyze legal and financial documents, address tax and probate concerns, and develop plans to minimize estate taxes while meeting your family's future needs. From estate preparation and tax planning to fiduciary accounting and income tax returns, we ensure your legacy is preserved and compliance requirements are met with ease.
